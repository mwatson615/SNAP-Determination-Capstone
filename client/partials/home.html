This app is accurate for Tennessee but does not include all criteria which could determine eligibility for the SNAP program. The only way to know for sure is to apply with your local DHS/SNAP office. This app does not account for household members who do not have social security numbers, who receive Social Security or SSI benefits, who receive income from sources other than an employer, or who have not been legal permanent residents for at least 5 years.
